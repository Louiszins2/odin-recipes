Pepperoni Pizza

DESCRIPTION:

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PREPARATION TIME : 40 min | SERVES: Six(06)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Pepperoni Pizza is a dish that many love and has an unmistakable flavor. In addition to the spicy flavor of salami,
the preparation can also include other ingredients, such as cheese and mushrooms.
It is a good choice to serve to friends or family.

Originating in Italy, it is a dish consisting of a generally round, flat base of wheat-based sourdough, topped with tomatoes,
cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as various types of sausage, anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives,
vegetables, meat, ham, etc.), which is then cooked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood oven.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

Contadina Quick Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 / 2 cup Water

  • 1 / 2 (12 ounce) can CONTADINA速 Tomate Paste

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed

  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed

  • 1 / 2 teaspoon garlic powder

  • 1 / 2 teaspoon onion powder

  • 1 / 2 teanspoon sugar

  • 1 / 2 teanspoon salt

  • 1 / 4 teaspoon black pepper

Fleischmann's Easy Pizza Crust:

  • 3 1 / 4 cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed

  • 2 (.25 ounce) envelopes FLEISCHMANN'S 速 Pizza Crust Yeast or RapidRise 速 Yeast

  • 1 teaspoon sugar

  • 1 1 / 2 teaspoons salt

  • 1 1 / 3 cups very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)

  • 1 / 3 cup oil

Toppings:

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste
  • 1 (6 ounce) package HORMEL 速 Pepperoni
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease two 12-inch pizza pans.

  2. Make sauce: Whisk together water, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and
    pepper in a medium bowl until smooth. Set aside.

  3. Make crust: Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and oil; mix until well blended,
    about 1 minute. Gradually add remaining flour, a little at a time, until a soft, sticky dough forms.

  4. Transfer dough to a floured surface; knead until dough is smooth and elastic, about 4 minutes.
    Add more flour as needed. (If using RapidRise yeast, let doug rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)

  5. Divide dough in half. Lightly flour your hands, then pat each piece of dough onto the prepared pizza pans.

  6. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly, 18 to 20 minutes. Rotate pizza
    pans between the top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
